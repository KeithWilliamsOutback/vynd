Platform features

Built for how vending
actually works

Every feature exists because a real vending operator needed it — not because it looked good on a roadmap.

🗺
Smart route management
Stop cards with fault alerts, fill status, cash estimates and bag tracking. Drivers see exactly what each stop needs before they arrive.
Driver app
📋
Fill guide with swipe gestures
Swipe right to confirm a slot, swipe left to record waste inline. Price mismatch flagging shows DEX vs VMS discrepancies per slot.
Driver app
📦
Stock pick & prekit
Minimum pick thresholds by unit or % of par. Fresh product prekit locks quantity to par and auto-records current stock as spoilage.
Both apps
💰
DEX price verification
Every slot flags when the machine's DEX-reported price differs from the VMS target. Driver is prompted to update label and keypad on the spot.
Driver app
📷
Machine photo capture
Configurable photo frequency — every fill, every Nth fill, or triggered automatically when a price change is activated on this visit.
Driver app
🏷
Cash bag scanning
Individual bag numbers per machine or a single route bag. Barcode scanning via BarcodeDetector API with manual fallback. Syncs to the cash screen live.
Driver app
🔢
SOH audit with DEX automation
Triggered every N fills. DEX vend data pre-calculates expected counts per slot — driver confirms or adjusts. Variances colour-coded by severity.
Driver app
🖼
Visual planogram editor
Tray-grouped slot grid with product images. Tab through Current, Par and Capacity fields across the whole machine. DEX view overlays price mismatches.
Management
🤝
Commission engine
Rules per customer — % of net cash, % of gross, or flat monthly fee. Applied at product family level. Monthly net revenue summary auto-calculated.
Management
⚖️
Reconciliation
DEX vend data matched against cash collected per machine per run. Variance highlighting, No DEX flagging, and open reconciliation tracking.
Management
🥬
Fresh product management
Life span in days triggers expiry alerts. Override prekit to par locks pick quantity and writes off current machine stock automatically on fill.
Both apps
🔍
Multi-barcode products
Primary barcode plus unlimited aliases per product. Essential for micromarket scanning where any barcode on the product must resolve to the same SKU.

How it works

One platform, two views

Management sets everything up. Drivers execute on the ground. Data flows both ways.

01
Manager sets up
Products, machines, planograms, pricing and commission rules configured in the management portal.
02
Route is built
Stops ordered, driver assigned, stock pick pre-calculated using current SOH vs par levels and min pick thresholds.
03
Driver executes
Pick, fill, audit, photograph, collect cash. Every action captured and time-stamped in the driver app.
04
Data reconciled
Cash collected matched to DEX vend data. Variances flagged, commission calculated, reports available immediately.
